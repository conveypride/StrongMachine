ABOUT US

Strong Machine Company Ltd. is your trusted partner in the world of heavy-duty machinery. With a primary focus on Chinese brands, particularly Shacman, SDLG, and Heli products, we specialize in the sales and servicing of robust equipment, including dump trucks, tipper trucks, forklifts, pallets, generators, and more. Our commitment to quality and reliability makes us your go-to source for powerful machinery solutions.

  • Specialization in heavy duty Chinese brands
  • Sales and servicing expertise for a wide range of equipment, including dump trucks, tipper trucks, forklifts, pallets, generators, and more.
  • Commitment to top-notch quality and reliability.

As your trusted partner in the industry, we check all the boxes to ensure you have access to powerful and dependable machinery solutions.
